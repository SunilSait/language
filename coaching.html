Coaching Programs

Structured Learning Tracks

Our language coaching follows a proven 4-phase immersion framework trusted by Fortune 500 companies and individual learners alike.

01
Needs Assessment & Baseline Test

A 30-minute diagnostic session to gauge current proficiency, learning goals, and preferred communication style.

02
Personalised Curriculum Design

Coaches build a custom syllabus covering industry vocabulary, grammar modules, pronunciation labs, and cultural etiquette guides.

03
Live Sessions + Immersion Exercises

Weekly 1-on-1 or group sessions via video, supported by real-world listening exercises, role-play simulations, and writing tasks.

04
Progress Review & Certification

Monthly milestone assessments lead to a LinguaBridge Proficiency Certificate upon program completion.
